🪨 Why These Hills Exist

Key Question: Why do some ridges stand up while others dissolve into rounded slopes?

The route climbs from Upper Cretaceous marine shale country near headquarters into forested terrain at Harlan. Soft Pierre Shale weathers into broad, rounded hills with slick footing when wet, while resistant sandstone caps or distant porphyry ridges hold the skyline.

🪶 Voices from the Land

This borderland between mountains and plains has long served as a travel corridor. Indigenous peoples moved seasonally through valleys and passes, using water sources as campsites. Travel respectfully and leave archaeological sites undisturbed.

Reflection: Imagine this landscape before roads and trails. How would you choose a camp and find water?
