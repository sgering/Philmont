# STYLE_GUIDE

## Visual Identity
- Theme: National Park Service meets USGS field guide.
- Tone: Educational, adventurous, timeless.

## Color Palette
- Forest Green (#2F5D3A)
- Granite Gray (#5C6166)
- Sand (#D8C6A3)
- Sky Blue (#5FA8D3)
- Rust (#A65A3A)
- Gold (#C49A32)

## Typography
- Headings: Montserrat or Source Sans Pro Bold
- Body: Source Serif Pro or Noto Serif
- Captions: Source Sans Pro
- Code/Data: JetBrains Mono

## Icons
Geology, Mining, Wildlife, Plants, History, Indigenous Culture, Astronomy, Weather, Photography, Scout Law.

## Layout
- Primary format: 11x17 landscape (collector)
- Field format: 5.5x8.5 portrait
- 12-column grid
- Consistent margins and spacing
- Use callout cards for facts and reflections.
