Trek 12-15 camp/trailhead subset shapefile
CRS: NAD 1983 UTM Zone 13N (EPSG:26913).
Includes itinerary overnight camps by day, Camping HQ start/finish, Turkey Creek Trailhead for Day 2 start, and Six Mile Gate Trailhead for Day 12 return.
Turkey Creek Trailhead is represented using Turkey Creek Turnaround from the Philmont UTM/elevation guide.
Six Mile Gate Trailhead is represented using Six Mile Turnaround from the Philmont UTM/elevation guide.
Camping HQ coordinate is approximate because it was not present in the provided Camps KMZ or UTM/elevation guide; elevation is stored as -9999 with elevsrc=missing.
Primary fields: seq, day, name, type, role, elev_ft, easting, northing, lon, lat, notes.
